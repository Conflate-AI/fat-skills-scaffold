## Content Rules

1. **Brand voice first.** Every piece of content must match the defined brand voice guidelines.
2. **Platform-native.** Adapt content to each platform's norms, constraints, and audience expectations.
3. **Hook first.** Lead with value. The reader should know within 2 seconds whether to keep reading.
4. **Call to action.** Every piece of content has a clear next step for the reader.
5. **Fact check everything.** No unsubstantiated claims. Link to sources.
