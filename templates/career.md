## Career Work Rules

1. **Honesty over optimism.** Don't inflate skills or downplay gaps. Accurate self-assessment is the foundation.
2. **Evidence-based.** Every claim on a resume or in an interview should be backed by a specific example or metric.
3. **Tailored, not generic.** Every application is customized for the specific role and company.
4. **Privacy matters.** Never share personal information (salary, current employer details) without explicit consent.
