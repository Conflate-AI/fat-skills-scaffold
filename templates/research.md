## Research Rules

1. **Cite everything.** Every claim must trace to a source. No unsupported assertions.
2. **Recency matters.** Note publication dates. Flag stale sources.
3. **Bias awareness.** Identify potential biases in sources and in your own synthesis.
4. **Distinguish fact from interpretation.** Make the boundary explicit.
5. **No redundant `cd`.** Working directory is already set.
